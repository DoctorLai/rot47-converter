# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-13_

The ROT47 Cipher Tool is designed to be private by default.

## No Data Collection

The tool runs **entirely in your browser**. The text you enter is processed
locally on your device and is **never** sent to any server. We do not collect,
store, or transmit your content.

## Local Storage

The app uses your browser's `localStorage` only to remember your preferences:

- your theme choice (light or dark mode), and
- your selected interface language.

This data stays in your browser, is never transmitted, and can be cleared at any
time via your browser settings.

## Cookies & Analytics

- We do **not** use cookies.
- We do **not** use analytics or any third-party tracking.

## Hosting

The site is hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/). As with any web
host, GitHub may log basic request metadata (such as IP addresses) for
operational and security purposes. See the
[GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)
for details.

## External Links

The app links to third-party sites (for example, GitHub, Buy Me a Coffee, and
[justyy.com](https://justyy.com)). Those sites have their own privacy policies,
which we do not control.

## Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected
in this file's history.
